About Me

Hey there! I'm Tiago, and I'm obsessed with data and human behavior. I love taking boring numbers and turning them into meaningful insights that can help businesses and people succeed.

I studied Psychology and Digital Psychology/Cyberpsychology (fancy way of saying mental health + tech), and I've used that knowledge to help translate complex questions into actionable insights. Basically, I'm the guy who can help you make sense of all that valuable data and communicate the results effectively to any audience!

Aside from data, I'm into video games, animals, and Zumba. In fact, I used to be a Zumba teacher - I know, right? I bring that same energy and enthusiasm to everything I do, including my work.

So, if you're looking for someone to help you optimize your processes, streamline your operations, and just make sense of your data, I'm your person! You can send me an email or check out my LinkedIn. Let's chat!
